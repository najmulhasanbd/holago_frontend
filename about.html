Our Story

HOW IT ALL STARTED

In April 2020, the idea of making a dream into reality was made possible to bridge the gap in fashion trends in Bangladesh. Holago was launched and started the production of top-quality clothing designs with the best fabric for the People of Bangladesh. The vision started when our CEO was in the final year of his university degree at the University of Greenwich, London. Being part of an active fashion community with different cultures and diversity, the difference in fashion trends between London and Dhaka seemed far apart. Whereas Bangladesh is one the top exporters of garments worldwide, locally these fashion trends and clothing solutions are unavailable to the regular public.

Our E-commerce platform started its operations in April 2020 taking our first online order. In the midst of the pandemic our services were available in the palm of everyones hands. We are thrilled to offer a wide range of stylish clothing options for men and women, at competitive prices. Our goal is to provide our customers with a convenient and hassle-free shopping experience.

We embarked on our journey keeping our consumers in mind. Consumers are willing to pay 5 times the price tag to wear clothing made by foreign brands as no industry made the effort to solve this problem. With the help of Fashion Designer Daniella Levis(London School of Fashion Graduate) from Portugal, Holago embarked on its mission to bring in top designs and quality fabrics to be produced in Bangladesh and distributed locally to bring about a revolution in Bangladeshs Fashion Trends. Aiming to go Global starting with The UK, Holago is built on the idea that we will satisfy our consumers with the latest trend in fashion and do so with affordable pricing.

HOLAGO LIFESTYLE BRAND, WHERE LOOK MATTERS.

A PERSONAL APPROACH

We are not just trying to sell clothes, we want to be part of your everyday lifestyle. With the help of top designers and fabric experts, we want every one of our products to bring out the best in you. Comfortable wear with a dashing look, we all are trying to achieve such in our daily lives. We are aiming to be your fashion advisor which is just a click away, no doubt everything at Holago has some backstory to it that suits your fashion needs.

TIME FOR A WARDROBE CHANGE

Shopping for what is best for you requires unnecessary effort in the Buzzing City of Dhaka, we believe the more context we add to our clothes the faster you can find what brings the best in you. That is why we have created the shop by occasion attribute so that your wardrobe has the outfit ready for you when the occasion calls. We are sending our special Style Match weekly newsletters to all our Holago club members, staying up to date on the latest trend in fashion. Feel the authenticity in your daily wear. Our promise is to keep you current with our styles, confident with the quality, and ready for action with our durability. Were here for you and were here to change your wardrobe for good. We have all been there before, where we do not know what is best for which occasion, what brings out the best in you. We are changing that. Keeping your wardrobe simple, yet sophisticated for every occasion.
